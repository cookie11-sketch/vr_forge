⚔ VR Forge — Quick Guide

📦 Inventory
Enter how many of each material you currently own. Use the +/− buttons or type directly. Materials are tracked per grade (White → Gold).
⚡ Smart Convert — pick a material and target grade to see the maximum you can reach using your current stock (fuse first, then dismantle). Enter a desired amount and click Convert to apply the changes automatically.
↑ Fuse — manually combine 4 of a lower grade into 1 higher grade.
↓ Dismantle — manually break 1 higher grade into 4 lower, at a gold cost.
⚙ Equipment
Browse all equipment. For each item you can:
  • Click a grade badge (White / Green / …) to mark it as crafted — this is required by the Checker as a prerequisite for higher grades.
  • Click Check to jump straight to the Checker for that item.
🔍 Checker
Select an equipment and a target grade to see if you can forge it:
  • Prerequisite — forging any grade above White requires the previous grade to already be crafted. The checker will tell you if the chain is clearable using your current materials.
  • Material table — shows each required material, how many you have, and whether you can meet the need directly, via fusing lower grades, or by dismantling higher ones (with gold cost).
  • When intermediate forge steps are simulated, quantities are shown after those steps so nothing is double-counted.
♻ Surplus
Set the equipment pieces you plan to craft (equipment, grade, and quantity), then see at a glance which materials you have enough of and which you're short on.
  • Click + Add Target to add a craft goal.
  • Pick the equipment, target grade, and how many copies you want.
  • The table below shows each required material with its surplus (+) or shortage (−) against your current inventory.
  • No targets set = no calculation shown. Start fresh and add only what you're working toward.
💾 All data is saved automatically in your browser's local storage. Use Export / Import on the Inventory page to back up or transfer your data.
